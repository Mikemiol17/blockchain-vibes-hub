Smart contracts are self-executing pieces of code stored on a blockchain. They automatically enforce rules and execute actions when predefined conditions are met ‚Äî no intermediaries required.

Smart contracts are written in programming languages like Solidity (for Ethereum) and deployed to the blockchain. Once deployed, they are immutable and transparent. Anyone can interact with them by sending transactions that trigger specific functions.

  • Escrow services for e-commerce and freelancing
  • Voting systems in DAOs (Decentralized Autonomous Organizations)
