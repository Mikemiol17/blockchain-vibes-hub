Layer 2 solutions are built on top of Layer 1 blockchains to improve scalability and reduce costs. They process transactions off-chain or in batches, then settle results back to Layer 1. Examples include Optimism, Arbitrum, zkSync, and StarkNet.

üìä Key Differences

  • Layer 1: Native security, slower throughput, higher fees
  • Layer 2: Faster transactions, lower fees, inherits Layer 1 security
  • Layer 2s scale Layer 1s without requiring protocol changes or hard forks

üí° Why It Matters

Layer 2s unlock mass adoption by making blockchain apps faster and cheaper. They allow developers to build scalable dApps while still relying on the security of Layer 1 networks.
